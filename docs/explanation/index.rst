Explanation
===========

Explanations provide a wider perspective of Imagecraft. They aid in understanding the
concepts and relationships of Imagecraft as a complete system.


Parts
-----

Parts are how software is brought into images. When an image is built, its parts are
processed in a series of ordered, reproducible steps.

- :ref:`explanation-parts`
- :ref:`lifecycle`
- :ref:`explanation-overlay-step`

Files travel as bundles through the parts lifecycle. These bundles are called
*filesets*.

:ref:`filesets_explanation`
